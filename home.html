CODE TOGETHER, STAY APART

CodeLab lets you seamlessly code like you're in the same room.

Features

Real Time Video Calls

CodeLab focuses on teaching students how to code instead of just troubleshooting their development machines. Forget fiddling around with clunkier tools like boring online courses, Skype, screen sharing - just teach using CodeLab.

Rich Messaging

CodeLab is what you need - a good editor and rich messaging in a chat room with your professor. Besides sending messages you have the ability to also send images and preview them right in the chat box.

Real time Feedback

With built in video and voice chat providing real time feedback, student's feel right in the classroom teaching. With our Replay Code capacity, CodeLab allows student to replay the lecture showing even the code they wrote in sequence along the video call.

Uses

Online Teaching

CodeLab helps courses to build around a creative theme and storyline to engage students making learning both educational and memorable. With courses covering C++, Java, JavaScript, HTML/CSS, Ruby, W2C pairs experienced instructors with meticulously produced, high-quality content help student to learn code remotely.

Empowering Girls

We aspire to stimulate young girls to achieve full potential in careers as computer science engineers. Computer programming is becoming an essential part in every engineer role and through CodeLab, young women can be provided mentorship and support to provide personal and professional development and encourage them to pursue a career in the field of engineering and computer science.

Code Interviews

After spending your time and energy to find a promising candidate, you bring them in for in-person interviews only to find out that they can't code or wouldn't fit too well with your team. CodeLab allows you to build a collaborative platform to take coding interview remotely, schedule interviews, send out calendar invites and share notes with your interviewers effortlessly from one convenient platform.
